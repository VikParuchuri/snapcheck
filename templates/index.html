Has your Snapchat info been leaked?

A few days ago, an exploit was published that allowed any phone number and username from Snapchat to be accessed. Today, someone released a full list of Snapchat users and their phone numbers. The last two digits of the numbers are removed, with the option to request the full numbers. This is scary, as it implies that the numbers could be sold.

Check if you were affected by entering your snapchat username or your phone number.
